Coyote Run Golf Course Picture Series: repba.com/?p=0098

This picture series displays the green area, hundred yard from green, and tee views of the 18 Leisure World Coyote Run Golf golf course holes. The holes to display are specified by the menu selections. The BINK application displays pictures at full height for all sized devices and alleviates the need for users to pinch out a browser image to provide full height  pictures as needed to achieve maximum picture clarity. It also provides convenient user viewing of the full width of pictures wider than the physical screen, auto slideshow capability, and picture magnification. 


Command Summary

-Click right/left side of picture to move to next/previous picture.

-When picture is stopped, swipe up/down to move left or right to see the full picture width when using small screen cellphone or tablet devices.

-Swipe left/right to initiate auto slideshow of pictures increasing/decreasing. Click anywhere on screen to stop the slideshow.

-Pinch the picture to enable or disable magnification where 1/3rd of the normal picture height is displayed on the full screen.
