Screen gestures provide motion interaction for burst image groups. The 10/sec 18-image golf motions are supported by panning, swiping, clicking. 

Pan up/down or swipe up/down to move to the previous/next image.

Click center, left, right of screen to start normal, slow, or fast motion.

Click anywhere on screen to stop motion.  

The right command menu icon provides Next, Zoom, or Help options.  Next initiates the next swing following the last image in the sequence.
