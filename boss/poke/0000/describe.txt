        Slideshow Rotations 
    </div>
    <div class="content">

        RepbaView portrait or landscape full height images are 
        displayed in Panorama or SlideShow motion. Depending on 
        image aspect ratio and user preference, one of the motion 
        types maybe preferred over the other. Panning tools enable 
        users to view the non displayed ends of landscape images 
        when they extend beyond the sides of the screen. After 
        clicking one of the following examples, click anywhere on 
        the upper image, then enable the full screen mode and 
        review the Panorama and Filmstrip displays.<p><br>

        Slideshow creates an image cylinder that can be
        rotated 360 degrees.  The rotation 
        motion is either panoramic or centered.  
        Panoramic rotation  pans the entire cylinder.   
        Wide images default to panoramic.
        Slideshow rotation centers each image.
        Portrait images default to slideshow.<p>

        Full screen mode resizes the image to the device's
        screen height.
        Image thumbnails of the slideshow can be viewed on the web page or in
        full screen mode.
        Start the full screen mode by tapping the image and then
        the full screen icon.
        Help is available in the full screen command menu.<p>

        Animated gifs are created for each image group.
        Gifs are useful when posting to social media and in emails. See 
        examples below.<p>
