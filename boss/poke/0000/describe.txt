        RepbaViewer Rotations 
    </div>
    <div class="content">

        RepbaViewer creates a panoramic image cylinder that can be rotated 360 degrees.
        The rotation is either panoramic or centered.
        Panoramic rotation pans the entire cylinder in both directions. 
        The initial rotation is determined by the url
        parameters.  Panoramas that consist of portrait images 
        default to a centered rotation. Short press or pan the panorama
        to rotate images when they extend beyond the screen. 
        Image thumbnails of the panorama are availale for fast selection.
        A no-motion option is available to avoid inadvertent rotation.<p><br>

        Animated GIFs are created for each panorama. Gifs are useful when
        posting to social media and emails. Click on the Gif examples below.
        These examples will take you directly to a full screen presentation.
