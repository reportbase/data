        RepbaView stores images in a cylinder format that can be rotated 360
        degrees to display panoramic or slideshow images.  The images are
        displayed by a rotating scan of the cylinder in either direction. The
        cylindrical image design is also used to implement RepbaView menus that
        provide convenient selection of image subgroups and documents.
        Image panorama or slideshow auto motion can be enabled at default or
        selectable speeds.  Manual screen gestures provide panning, movement of
        images, and start/stop auto motion.  User screen controls also
        allow users to view the hidden portions of landscape images.  Short
        press or pan the image when it extends beyond the horizontal ends of
        the screen.  Image thumbnails, as shown by the top image, are available
        for fast search and image selection.  A no-auto-motion option is available
        to avoid inadvertent auto rotation during manual interaction.<p><br>

        Repba also creates animated GIFs when building imagegroups. Gifs are
        useful for posting to social media and emails. Click on the following GIF images
        to enable various imagegroups. For each imagegroup, view different subgroups
        by selecting the left image menu.
