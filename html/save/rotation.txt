    <div class="content">

        RepbaView creates a panoramic image cylinder that can be rotated 360
        degrees.  The rotation is either panoramic or centered.  Panoramic
        rotation scans the complete cylinder in both directions.  Panoramas that
        consist of portrait images default to a centered rotation. Short press
        or pan the panorama when an image extends beyond the physical 
        boundry of the screen.  Image thumbnails, as shown by the top image,
        are available for fast search and image selection.
        A no-motion option is available to avoid inadvertent
        rotation.<p><br>

        Animated GIFs are created for each panorama. Gifs are useful when
        posting to social media and emails. Click on the Gif examples below.
        These examples will take you directly to a full screen presentation.
