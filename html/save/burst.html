FSPIV Burst View Help

Screen gestures provide motion interaction for burst image groups. The 10/sec 18-image motions are supported by panning, swiping, clicking, and pinching. 

Pan up/down or swipe up/down to move to the previous/next image.

Click center, left, right of screen to start normal, slow, or fast motion.

Click anywhere on screen to stop motion.  

Zoom to top, middle, or bottom 1/3 of screen by pinching the desired area to zoom. Pinch anywhere on screen to revert image to normal size.

The right command menu icon provides Next, Zoom, or Help options.  Next initiates the next image sequence following the last image in the sequence.  The top left menu icon enables viewing specific image sequences.
